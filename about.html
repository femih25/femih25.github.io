About me

HUMANITY, CLARITY, VISUAL ENGAGEMENT

As a student at Northwestern University studying journalism, data science and design, the throughline guiding everything I do is my goal of making the complex feel both clear and deeply human.

No story or problem looks the same from each angle, and the way we present information shapes how it's understood.

What multimedia elements help a complicated story resonate? How can a design feel intuitive and inviting instead of overwhelming? How do typography, color and structure work together to earn someone's attention — and keep it?

I'm especially drawn to data and visual journalism, as well as digital media design that meets people exactly where they are. If that interests you, please reach out!
